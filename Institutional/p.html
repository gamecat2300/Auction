Qualification Requirements:

Brokers that bring any qualifying asset(s) to Auction.com with a signed Marketing Agreement by 10/19/15 will receive $5,000. If that same asset sells at auction then the broker will receive an additional $10,000 upon closing.
Requirements:

  • A Marketing Agreement must be fully executed between 8/12/15 and 10/19/15 to receive the first $5,000.

  • The additional payment of $10,000 will be paid upon closing of the sale of the asset, so long as the asset sells at auction by 12/31/2015.

  • Property value of $2 million & above, as determined by the Auction.com Valuations Team.

  • All commercial property types other than land will be considered.

  • Planned auction date must be between 10/1/15 and 12/31/15.

  • Multiple assets can be submitted, each qualifying asset is eligible for up to $15,000 in total referral fees.

  • Submitter must hold a valid real estate broker license.

  • Broker must complete and submit a form W-9 to receive payment.

  • Any asset with a Marketing Agreement signed prior to 8/12/15 will not qualify for this promotion.

  • The qualifying broker must not be the seller.
